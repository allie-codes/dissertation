{% trans "Soil Sampling Instructions" %}

{% trans "You will need:" %}

  • {% blocktrans %}10 zip-lock sandwich bags (non-compostable/non-biodegradable bags so they won't break before testing){% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}A permanent marker (or self-adhesive labels and pen){% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}A trowel or scoop{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}A box or bag to transport or post your samples{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}To collect your soil sample:{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Step 1{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Label five zip-lock bags with the 8-digit participant ID number provided to you after you submitted your participation form and sample ID.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}If you have misplaced your participant ID number, visit the {% endblocktrans%}{% trans "results" %}{% blocktrans %} page to find it.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Sample IDs match your consent form as follows:{% endblocktrans %}

  • {% blocktrans %}F-1: front garden{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}B-1: back garden near house (0.5 to 2 meters away){% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}D-1: patch most distant to buildings{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}V-1: veggie patch #1{% endblocktrans %}
  • {% blocktrans %}V-2: veggie patch #2{% endblocktrans %}
{% blocktrans %}Step 2{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}At your first location, collect soil from the top 5 cm over a 20 x 20 cm area using a trowel or scoop. Your sample should be the size of a cricket/tennis ball.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Step 3{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Place soil (reasonably dry, without any plant material and stones, please) into the correctly labelled zip-lock bag and seal.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Step 4{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Repeat at the remaining locations. If you don't have all of the locations, don't worry!{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Step 5{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}'Double bag' each sample to protect the labels and prevent spillage.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Step 6{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Place your samples in a bag or box clearly labelled 'EcoGardenHealth' and drop in at the University (see map below). Alternatively, you can post your samples to us at the address below.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Please make sure you have correctly labeled your samples and filled in the online questionnaire/consent form before sending us your samples.{% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Thank you!{% endblocktrans %}
